Ease is a greater threat to progress than hardship

Writing and performing poems in high school allowed for me to use my creative abilities, in retrospect, I did not know that I had. My abilities have found a new home in not only writing code, but also a newfound interest in using different software techniques and tools to extract knowledge from data, both structured and unstructured. This newfound love is relatively new in the sense that it was discovered through research opportunities and internships at both Elizabeth City State University (ECSU), Texas State University, and NASA Langley Research Center (LaRC) in Hampton, Virginia.
